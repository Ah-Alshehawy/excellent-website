Why Egypt

Egypt is the largest consumer market in the Arab world and serves as a strategic entry point for international brands seeking to establish a foothold in the MENA region.

With a young, ambitious population, an expanding middle class, and ongoing economic development, Egypt offers international brands significant long-term growth potential alongside near-term operational opportunity.
